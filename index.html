Korean Skincare Philosophy

Korean skincare is built on a foundation of gentle, consistent care focused on hydration, prevention, and addressing skin concerns with targeted ingredients.

The Philosophy

Prevention First

Korean approach focuses on preventing skin issues rather than treating them after they appear.

Hydration is Key

Multiple hydrating layers create deeply moisturized, plump skin that's better able to heal and protect itself.

Gentle is Better

Harsh ingredients and quick fixes are avoided in favor of gentle formulations used consistently over time.

The Steps Explained

1. Oil Cleanser

Removes oil-based impurities like sunscreen, makeup and sebum.

2. Water-Based Cleanser

Removes water-based impurities like sweat and dirt.

3. Exfoliator

Removes dead skin cells to improve texture and product absorption.

4. Toner

Balances skin pH and preps for following treatments.

5. Essence

Lightweight treatment that hydrates and enhances skin repair.

6. Treatments (Serums/Ampoules)

Concentrated formulas targeting specific skin concerns.

7. Sheet Mask

Intense hydration and treatment delivery (typically 2-3 times weekly).

8. Eye Cream

Targeted treatment for the delicate eye area.

9. Moisturizer

Seals in previous treatments and provides lasting hydration.

10. Sunscreen (AM)/Sleeping Pack (PM)

Final protection from UV damage during day or intensive overnight treatment.

Note: Your personalized routine includes the steps most beneficial for your specific skin concerns and preferences. Not every routine needs all 10 steps!
